About

Sugar for Ants started with producers Paulo Gouvea and Leo Clark with the intention of mixing full-on psytrance with cool old jazz tunes from Miles Davis and Stephane Grapelli.

Download demo remixes and a sample from "The Psyjazz tapes" below. Enjoy — we're still learning to prepare Sugar :)
